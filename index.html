Jewel Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There will be four crystals below. By pressing on a crystal you will add a specific amount to your total score. You will win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value of the crystal will be hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
